# Ali Hariri Movahed

CS + Math at RWTH Aachen, somewhere between student research and founding.

I work at the seam where machine learning meets the physical world: physics-informed models that respect the equations they're trained on, perception pipelines that run under hard real-time constraints, and autonomous agents that do real work rather than demo well. Some of it becomes a paper at a NeurIPS workshop; some of it becomes a product — most recently a system where AI maintains and improves live websites on its own, and the human only signs off.

Some of what I build is public, most of it isn't yet.
